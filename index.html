How it works

A living space to capture early signals, let ideas breathe, and surface the strongest ones into the formal FEI pipeline. Everyone contributes; the FEI team synthesizes.

1

Submit

Anyone — L&D Brand, Industrial Design, FEI pods — can add an idea using the "Add idea" tab. No polish required. A rough consumer signal, a retail observation, or a trend you can't stop thinking about all qualify.

2

Incubate

Ideas sit in the bank until the next cross-functional review. Others can react and add context. Nothing gets deleted — only archived when a formal decision is made.

3

Synthesize

The FEI team reviews top ideas with L&D Brand and flags candidates for deeper exploration or Create/Synthesize sessions. DFV signals help the group prioritize quickly without lengthy debate.

4

Advance or stop

High-potential ideas are developed into FEI opportunity briefs and socialized with brand teams. Others are archived with rationale documented for future reference.

2026 goal targets

  • Facilitate 2+ Create/Synthesize sessions with FEI pods; ≥25% O&R and Household FEI members
  • Maintain ≥10 top ideas in this living document
  • Socialize ≥1 FEI-originated opportunity brief with L&D brand teams
