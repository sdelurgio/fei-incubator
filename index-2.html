How it works

A living space to capture early signals, let ideas breathe, and surface the strongest ones into the formal FEI pipeline. Everyone contributes; the FEI team synthesizes monthly.

1

Submit

Anyone — L&D Brand, Industrial Design, FEI pods — can submit an idea using the "Add idea" tab. A Microsoft Form opens in a new tab. No polish required — rough signals, retail observations, and consumer quotes all welcome.

2

Collect

Submissions are automatically saved to a shared Excel file on the FEIPOD SharePoint site. Anyone with access can view responses as they come in.

3

Synthesize

Each month, the FEI team reviews all submissions, selects the strongest ideas, and updates this Idea Bank. DFV signals help prioritize quickly without lengthy debate.

4

Advance or stop

High-potential ideas are developed into FEI opportunity briefs and socialized with L&D brand teams. Others are archived with rationale documented for future reference.
